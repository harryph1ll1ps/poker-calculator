# Poker Calculator (Zero AI)
## Tech stack
- Typescript
- React native

## Plan
- You can repeatedly simulate a full hand of poker (Texas Holdem). 
- Throughout the match, you can see the odds that your hand has of winning. 
- You can select the number of players [2,10]
- It is all mathematical, the other bots play (i.e. check, raise) or fold based on the odds of them winning.
- In the first instance we do not worry about the actual pot, it is simply tracking the likelihood that your card combination would win.

### UI/UX
- React native web app (local)
- You can play in two modes: open/closed
    - Open: you can see the other cards and percentages
    - Closed: you can only see your cards
- Your options are fold/play
    - It gives you feedback based on whether you made the statistically favourable move

### Database
- SQLite
